YouTube Music Widget Redesign

Improving mobile music control accessibility for on-the-go listening

Product Design UI/UX Mobile Figma Personal Project

Problem

Key features buried deep in settings

Solution

Redesigned widgets with quick-access controls

Impact

Faster access to equalizer & track scrubbing

Role

Product Designer

Context

As an audiophile, I've been an avid user of portable music players spanning various form factors. When the medium changed from physical to digital, many components transitioned over.

Over time, I've settled on YouTube Music as my default player because of its customization to my preferences. On Android, it allows access to an equalizer as well as the ability to upload your own library.

Unfortunately, some features are buried deep in the settings. This can be especially hard when you need to quickly access these features while on the go.

Problem

As a user who uses the product on the go, I would like a more minimal interface with the opportunity to expand to view other features and options.

For example, I would like the choice of seeking a track forward and back by 10-15 seconds, and faster access to the equalizer without diving through multiple menus.

Solution: Redesigned Widgets

In the redesign, I focused on two key improvements:

  • Quick access to the equalizer without navigating through settings
  • Added track rewind and forward scrubber icons that allow you to quickly rewind or seek forward by 10 seconds
  • Maintained the minimal interface with expandable options
  • Improved visual hierarchy for better scannability

Takeaways

This personal project reinforced the importance of designing for real-world use cases. As someone who uses YouTube Music daily while commuting, I identified pain points that many mobile users likely experience.

By focusing on quick-access controls and maintaining a minimal interface, the redesign improves the user experience without adding visual clutter. The expandable widget concept allows users to choose their level of control based on their immediate needs.
